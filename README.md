## ğŸ“ˆ Dashboard Insights

### 1) Sales Trend by Establishment Year
- Older outlets (e.g., 2018) show higher sales (~$205K).
- A declining trend is visible for edge years (e.g., 2011 at ~$78K).
- Indicates maturity and customer trust impact sales performance.

### 2) Outlet Size Contribution
- Medium outlets contribute the highest share (~$507.9K).
- Followed by Small (~$444.7K) and High (~$248.9K).
- Medium-sized outlets are the most profitable format.

### 3) Outlet Tier Performance
- Tier 3 leads with ~$472K in sales.
- Tier 2 and Tier 1 follow.
- Suggests stronger demand in Tier 3 locations.

### 4) Item Type Analysis (by Avg Sales)
Top performing categories:
- Household
- Dairy
- Starchy Foods
- Snack Foods
- Fruits & Vegetables

Lower performers:
- Baking Goods
- Health & Hygiene
- Soft Drinks

### 5) Fat Content Preference
- Regular and Low Fat items contribute almost equally to sales.
- Tier-wise variation shows changing customer preference by location.
